Contential Divide Trek

Test your edurance!!

The Contential Divide Trek happens only once per year!

In the beautiful fall scenery, expert cyclist pit their skills against the problematic trek across Montana.

This 25-day trek covers the length of Montana.

These expert bicyclists start at the Montana/Wyoming border and finish at the Idaho state line.

For only $500, you will receive a fantastic certificate and bragging rights.
